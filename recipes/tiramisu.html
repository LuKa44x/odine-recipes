Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that is made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a rich, velvety mascarpone cream. It is a decadent and indulgent treat that is perfect for special occasions or a sweet ending to a meal.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers, Espresso, Mascarpone cheese, Eggs, Sugar, Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Prepare the espresso and allow it to cool.
  2. Beat the egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  3. Whisk in the mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  5. Fold the egg whites into the mascarpone mixture.
  6. Dip the ladyfingers into the espresso and layer them in a dish.
  7. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  8. Repeat the layers and refrigerate for several hours.
  9. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
