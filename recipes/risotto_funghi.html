Risotto ai funghi

Description

Risotto ai funghi is a creamy and savory Italian rice dish that is made with Arborio rice, mushrooms, and a variety of seasonings. It is a comforting and satisfying meal that is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice, Mushrooms, Onion, Garlic, White wine, Broth, Parmesan cheese, Butter, Seasonings

Steps

  1. Saut√© the onion and garlic in butter until soft.
  2. Add the mushrooms and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the rice and cook until translucent.
  4. Deglaze with white wine and cook until absorbed.
  5. Add the broth, one ladle at a time, stirring until absorbed.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and seasonings.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
